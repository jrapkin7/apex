  • VO₂ bike intervals — 1×/wk once cleared: 4–6 × 1-min hard / 2-min easy. Bike protocol for the hip.

Rule of thumb: protein first at every meal, even without appetite — it's the muscle you keep while the fat comes off. Fiber and vegetables at each meal work your lipids and insulin at the same time.

Grocery staples to prep ahead

  • Protein: chicken, fish/salmon, eggs, Greek yogurt, cottage cheese, whey, lean beef, jerky.
  • Fiber & carbs: beans, lentils, berries, oats, potatoes, quinoa, loads of vegetables.
  • Fats: olive oil, avocado, nuts, seeds — favor unsaturated for the lipids.
  • Prep once: batch-cook protein + a big tray of roasted veg on Sunday — hitting 200g protein gets easy.

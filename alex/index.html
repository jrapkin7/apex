Cardio & zones

  • Zone 2 — 2–3×/wk, 20–30 min easy (walk/bike, conversational pace). Heart health + recovery.
  • Zone 4–5 (optional) — 1×/wk 6 × 30-sec bike sprints. Builds VO₂.

Nutrition — hit your targets

  • Pre-workout: eggs + oats with banana & honey (~600 cal / 35P).
  • Post-workout lunch (biggest): chicken + 1.5 cups rice + veg (~760 cal / 58P).
  • Dinner: family dinner (protein + carb + veg) — 2 palms protein, 2–3 cupped carbs.
  • Evening: protein Ninja Creami (~500 cal / 40P).
  • Fill: Greek yogurt + granola to top up on big days.
